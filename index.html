Places to Visit

Eiffel Tower: Paris

The Eiffel Tower is one of the most iconic landmarks in the world, located in the heart of Paris, France. Standing tall at 330 meters, this iron lattice tower offers breathtaking views of the city and is a must-visit for travelers from all over the globe.

Great Wall: China

The Great Wall of China, an ancient series of walls and fortifications, stretches across the northern borders of China. Built to protect Chinese states from invasions, it is a marvel of engineering and a testament to human perseverance.

Taj Mahal: India

The Taj Mahal, an exquisite white marble mausoleum, is a symbol of love and a UNESCO World Heritage site. Located in Agra, India, it attracts millions of visitors each year who come to admire its stunning architecture and historical significance.
